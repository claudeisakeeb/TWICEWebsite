Nayeon (September 22, 1995)

  • Role(s): Lead Vocalist, Lead Dancer, Center
  • Face of the group
  • Known as the "Fake Maknae" for her bubbly personality
  • Has a dog named Kookeu
  • Secretly auditioned behind her parents' backs, got accepted by JYPE in 2010, and became a trainee for 5 years
  • Was initially set to debut in a group called "6mix" with current members Jeongyeon and Jihyo, but that project was cancelled
  • First member to be announced on "SIXTEEN" t o be a member of TWICE
  • Huge fangirl of IU and SNSD's Taeyeon
  • Almost always live-sings her parts at concerts/award shows
  • Her aegyo acts (most notably on Running Man) have gone viral several times
  • Best friends with Jeongyeon and very good friends with Jihyo (Known as 3mix)
  • Contributed to lyrics for 24/7 (twicetagram), RAINBOW (Feel Special), and 21:29 (Feel Special)

Jeongyeon (November 1, 1996)

  • Role(s): Lead Vocalist
  • Obsessed with cleanliness
  • Dislikes doing and watching aegyo (especially Nayeon's)
  • Prefers short hair but grew her hair out to "show another side of herself to ONCE"
  • Passed a JYPE audition in 2010 became a JYPE trainee for five years before appearing on survival reality show "SIXTEEN"
  • Was accepted by both SM Entertainment and JYPE, but chose JYPE in the end
  • Was initially set to debut in a group called "6mix" with current members Nayeon and Jihyo, but that project was cancelled
  • Best friends with Nayeon and very good friends with Jihyo (Known as 3mix)
  • Huge fangirl of IU and SNSD's Taeyeon
  • Extremely close and gets along well with Mina
  • Contributed to lyrics for Love Line (twicetagram), Sweet Talker (Summer Nights), LaLaLa (YES or YES) , and 21:29 (Feel Special)

Momo (November 9, 1996)

  • Role(s): Main Dancer, Sub Vocalist, Rapper (sometimes)
  • Jokbal fanatic
  • Loves cooking and eating
  • Was scouted with her sister after JYPE agents saw one of their dance videos
  • Passed the 2011 JYPE audition, but her sister did not
  • Started her trainee years in 2012 with current TWICE memberSana
  • Widely accepted across the kpop industry as one of the its best dancers
  • Was originally eliminated from SIXTEEN but joined TWICE because JYPE thought her dancing skills were invaluable to the group
  • Trended above a 2019 Apple Event with of a teaser video that showed her without bangs
  • Part of the J-Line (Sana, Mina, Momo)
  • Mentioned on Knowing Bros that she's dumber than before due to a childhood head injury
  • Contributed to lyrics for Shot Thru the Heart (Summer Nights), HOT (FANCY), 21:29 (Feel Special), and LOVE FOOLISH (Feel Special)

Sana (December 29, 1996)

  • Role(s): Sub Vocalist
  • Shy Shy Shy
  • TWICE's variety show ACE
  • Was scouted on the streets of Japan by JYPE agents while shopping with one of her friends
  • Started her trainee years in 2012 with current TWICE memberMomo
  • The most bubbly and child-like member
  • Known for her aegyo (much like Nayeon)
  • Part of the J-Line (Sana, Mina, Momo)
  • Clumsiest member, it's noted that she often stumbles around the dorm and is not good with balance.
  • Famously danced to SNSD's Mr. Taxi for her JYPE audition, something the other members still make fun of her for to this day
  • Contributed to lyrics for Shot Thru the Heart (SumNi), Turn It Up (FANCY), and 21:29 (Feel Special)

Jihyo (February 1, 1997)

  • Role(s): Leader, Main Vocalist
  • Her birth name was Park Ji-Soo, but she legalized it to Park Ji-hyo at age 18
  • Was a JYPE trainee for 10 years (Started at age 8 in 2005 after being scouted for JYPE for getting 2nd on a Junior Naver competition)
  • Dealt with and overcame being overweight and "not being fit enough for camera" during her trainee/early debut years
  • Nearly always live-sings her parts during concerts and award shows
  • Has her aegyo moments
  • Was initially set to debut in a group called "6mix" with current members Nayeon and Jeongyeon, but that project was cancelled
  • Comedically noted to resemble Thomas the Tank Engine
  • Was revealed to have been dating Kang Daniel since the beginning of 2019
  • Contributed to lyrics for 24/7 (twicetagram), Eyes Eyes Eyes (SIGNAL), HO! (Summer Nights), Sunset (YES or YES), Girls Like Us (FANCY), GET LOUD (Feel Special), and 21:29 (Feel Special)

Mina (March 24, 1997)

  • Role(s): Main Dancer, Sub Vocalist
  • Her English-born name is Sharon (Born in Texas)
  • Her English is noted to be the best among all TWICE members, but she prefers to not speak it
  • Part of the J-Line (Sana, Mina, Momo)
  • Practied ballet for 11 years prior to joining TWICE (known as Black Swan)
  • Known as the "Queen of Bridges" to her fans due to singing the bridges in most TWICE songs
  • Softest and most angelic voice in the group
  • Most introverted member
  • Can't do aegyo
  • Signature color is mint green, popularized by the #GetWellSoonMina movement during TWICELIGHTS
  • Contributed to lyrics for Shot Thru the Heart (Summer Nights) and 21:29 (Feel Special)

Dahyun (March 28, 1998)

  • Role(s): Lead Rapper, Sub Vocalist
  • Widely known as Dubu (Tofu)
  • Was a trainee for a little more than three years before debuting
  • Went viral on Youtube in 6th grade with an Eagle Dance
  • School Meal Club Member
  • Extremely proficient at piano
  • Part of the popular TWICE ship SaiDa
  • Excels at crackhead dancing
  • Long time friends with Chaeyoung
  • It was mentioned in an interview that she cares the most about ONCE out of all the members
  • Contributed to lyrics for Missing U (twicetagram), 21:29 (Feel Special), and TRICK IT (Feel Special)

Chaeyoung (April 23, 1999)

  • Role(s): Main Rapper, Sub Vocalist
  • Known as the Strawberry Princess (She owns a strawberry farm)
  • Earned the nickname "Baby Lion" for resembling "Simba" from "The Lion King"
  • Auditioned for JYPE in 2012 and became a trainee for a little less than 3 years before appearing on survival reality show "SIXTEEN" and joining TWICE
  • Expressed interest in branching out from TWICE's "cute" concepts in 2018
  • Known to draw in her free time
  • School Meal Club Member
  • Cut her hair short without warning after thinking Kristen Stewart looked cool with short hair; the company told her to at least say something if she wanted to do something like that
  • Long time friends with Dahyun and close with IOI's (former) Somi
  • Contributed to lyrics for Precious Love (PAGE TWO), Eyes Eyes Eyes (SIGNAL), Missing U (twicetagram), Don't Give Up (twicetagram), Sweet Talker (Summer Nights), Young & Wild (YES or YES), Strawberry (FANCY), and 21:29 (Feel Special)

Tzuyu (June 14, 1999)

  • Role(s): Lead Dancer, Sub Vocalist, Visual
  • Has a dog named Gucci
  • Scouted at a performing workshop in Taiwan in 2012 and became a trainee for a little more than two years before appearing on survival reality show "SIXTEEN" and joining TWICE, effectively making her the member with the least amount of training
  • Was originally eliminated from SIXTEEN but joined TWICE with popular vote
  • The youngest but most hard working member
  • School Meal Club Member
  • Mentioned that she has trouble expressing herself in front of strangers
  • Can be fairly blunt but has all the best intentions
  • Won second place on TC Candler's annual "Most Beautiful Faces" list in 2018
  • Close friends with CLC's Elkie
  • Contributed to lyrics for 21:29 (Feel Special)
