PAGE TWO

April 25, 2016


Track List:


  • CHEER UP
  • Precious Love
  • Touchdown
  • Tuk Tok
  • Woohoo
  • My Headphones on
  • I'm gonna be a star (CD ver ONLY)

"PAGE TWO" is TWICE's second extended play. The album was praised for its songs' "high-spirited and cheerful" energy and being "generally refreshing and witty.""Touchdown" in particular was praised for its powerful sound and energetic feeling. Critique for the album was mainly directed at a lack of diversity in its sounds.


Fun Fact #1: CHEER UP won the "Song of the Year" daesang at the 2016 MAMA Awards


Fun Fact #2: CHEER UP is TWICE's longest charting song on MelOn's Top 100 (68 weeks)

Twicecoaster: Lane 1

October 24, 2016


Track List:


  • TT
  • 1 to 10
  • Ponytail
  • Jelly Jelly
  • Pit-A-Pat
  • Next Page
  • One in a Million

"Twicecoaster: Lane 1" is TWICE's third extended play. The EP shot TWICE into international fame and cemented them as the top selling girl group of 2016. TWICE won their first music show award with "TT" and performed on Show Champion for the first time since their debut.


Fun Fact #1: "TT" is TWICE's most viewed music video. As of September 2019, it has over 477 million views.


Fun Fact #2: The album reportedly sold more than 165,000 physical copies on Gaon chart within a week, beating PAGE TWO's five-month sales record in only 7 days.


Fun Fact #3: "One in a Million" eventually became TWICE's catchphrase.

Signal

May 15, 2017


Track List:


  • SIGNAL
  • THREE TIMES A DAY
  • ONLY YOU
  • HOLD ME TIGHT
  • EYES EYES EYES
  • SOMEONE LIKE ME

"Signal" is TWICE's fourth extended play and was the first EP that TWICE actively promoted on variety shows. Its signature dance move became iconic in the kpop industry and is still widely prevalent in the industry today. This EP is also the first one to have lyrics written by TWICE members with Jihyo and Chaeyoung writing the lyrics for "EYES EYES EYES."


Fun Fact: "SIGNAL" won the "Song of the Year" daesang award at the 2017 MAMA show.

"#TWICE" is TWICE's first Japanese album and consits of both the Japanese and Korean versions of TWICE's title tracks. #TWICE affirmed TWICE's popularity in Japan and was the spearhead of the TWICE-led hallyu wave into Japanese culture.


Fun Fact: "#TWICE" sold over 260,000 copies, which earned it a Platinum certification by the Recording Industry Association of Japan (RIAJ).

FANCY YOU

April 22, 2019


Track List:


  • FANCY
  • STUCK IN MY HEAD
  • GIRLS LIKE US
  • HOT
  • TURN IT UP
  • STRAWBERRY

After a well deserved break, "FANCY YOU" was released as TWICE's seventh extended play. TWICE members and multiple industry sources mentioned that the concept for "FANCY" was going to show a more "mature" side of TWICE that fans haven't seen in their previous releases. The album garnered thousands of international fans and expanded the TWICE fandom massively across the international stage. Although music reception was extremely positive, there was online criticism directed at lackluster album art and teasers.


Fun Fact: "FANCY"'s music video was TWICE's first to garner 40+ million views within 24 hours of its online release.
